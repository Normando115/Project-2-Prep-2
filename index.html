Dancing ball - Artist Statement

My concept for this is a dancing a ball in a dark room. The floor changes color to give the room

some depth and the ball colors rotate. This project was my most confusing one as I went into this

project late and what I wanted to do was very difficult as there were many errors along the way from

the beginning to the very end. I originally wanted to do a disco room with flashing lights and gradual

changes but that didn't happen because after days of no progress I changed to this. My intentions for

making the dancing ball was to emulate what its like for someone to practice for hours doing the same

thing over and over to perfection practice for hours doing the same thing over and over to perfection.
